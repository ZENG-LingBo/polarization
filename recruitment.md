# Recruitment — Cautions, Screener, and Messages (EN / 中文)

## ⚠️ Recruitment cautions (read before posting anything)

**Methodological**
1. **Mask the true purpose.** Never mention "polarization," "conflict," "reduce
   hostility," "AI / bots," or "intervention" in recruitment. Neutral cover story:
   *"how NBA fans discuss their teams online."* Revealing the goal causes self-selection
   (agreeable people opt in) and demand characteristics.
2. **Recruit identified fans, not casuals** — gate on SSIS ≥ midpoint
   (`survey_instrument.md`). No identity → no polarization to defuse.
3. **Balanced quotas** ~50/50 Lakers/Celtics; over-recruit ~30% for no-shows in
   synchronous sessions.
4. **Do not reveal the AI agents** — that's for the debrief, not recruitment.
5. **Anti-fraud:** attention checks + a fan-knowledge check + device/IP de-dup.

**Ethical / IRB**
6. **Deception (undisclosed agents) needs IRB approval + a debrief** with the option to
   withdraw data afterward. Consent flags, without spoiling: *"some aspects will be
   explained at the end."*
7. **Disclose** possible exposure to heated/uncivil content; voluntary; withdraw anytime;
   provide a cooldown + support resource.
8. **18+ only** (exclude minors explicitly).
9. **Privacy:** logging identity-linked (possibly toxic) posts → de-identify, secure
   storage, explicit data-use consent; **GDPR (EU) / PIPL (mainland China)** if relevant.
10. **Respect platform ToS** — get moderator permission before posting in fan
    subreddits / Discords / Hupu; don't spam.

**Cross-cultural**
11. Chinese vs. Western fans differ in politeness norms and in what the rivalry means;
    if mixing, analyze arms separately or restrict. **Decide the discussion language** —
    English platform needs English-fluent participants; otherwise run a parallel
    Chinese-language arm (Hupu 虎扑 / Weibo / RedNote).

---

## Screener (gate before enrollment)

- Age **≥ 18**.
- Supports **Lakers OR Celtics** (exclude "another team / none / both").
- Follows the NBA regularly.
- **SSIS ≥ midpoint** (genuine identification).
- Language fluency matching the assigned arm.
- Passes attention + fan-knowledge check (e.g., a basic current-roster item).
- Available for a **~45–60 min synchronous session** + a short follow-up ~1–2 weeks later.
